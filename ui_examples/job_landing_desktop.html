At the top of Google's core principles is: "Focus on the user and all else will follow." So, it’s no surprise that we share the same passion for innovation and excellence in design as we do in engineering. We believe each Google product should have an intuitive, simple and beautiful design that delights users each time they visit—regardless of whether they use a desktop computer, mobile device, Google Glass or other hardware.

Our team of User Experience (UX) Designers, Interaction Designers, Visual Designers, Motion Designers, Production Designers and Producers work in a fun, collaborative and fast-paced environment. They constantly refine Google’s visual design language, tackle complex user interactions and highlight each product’s brand and unique personality.

Requirements

  • BA/BS degree or equivalent practical experience.
  • Experience at a creative agency or an in-house team with an awe-inspiring portfolio of brand-defining interactive experiences.
  • Experience managing agencies and building teams.
  • Knowledge of design trends, as well as an understanding of consumer insights.
  • A collaborative approach that fits with the Google spirit.

Responsibilities

  • Deliver strategically sound, conceptually based, iconic experiences whilst being a brand catalyst, creating epic opportunities for the Google Brand.
  • Be a strategic partner to our product, marketing, development and Creative Lab functions.
  • Lead and mentor a team of web designers, information architects and UX designers. Lead external experience, design and production agencies.
  • Manage a large body of work in a fast-paced, ambiguous environment while maintaining Google's reputation for reliability.
  • Evangelise beauty and craft.
